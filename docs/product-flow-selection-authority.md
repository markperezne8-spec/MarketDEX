# Product Flow Selection Authority

Inventory selection is the authority for downstream operator workspaces.

- Inventory remains available at all times.
- Pricing is unavailable until one active inventory asset is selected.
- Listing Workflow is unavailable until one active inventory asset is selected.
- Clearing selection returns the operator to Inventory and locks downstream workspaces again.

This keeps MarketDEX aligned to the operator flow: Inventory → Pricing → Listing Workflow.
