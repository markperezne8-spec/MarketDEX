# Direct Workspace Tab Access Fix

Pricing and Listing Workflow are operator workspaces and must remain directly reviewable from their tabs.

Inventory selection continues to control the guided `Continue to Pricing` and `Continue to Listing Workflow` handoff buttons. It no longer disables the workspace tabs or forces the operator back to Inventory when selection clears.

This preserves guided asset context without making primary MarketDEX pages appear broken or inaccessible.
