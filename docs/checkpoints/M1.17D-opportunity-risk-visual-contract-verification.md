- local-only prepared or injected evidence only;
- missing evidence renders `Unavailable` instead of invented opportunity, risk, urgency, or live status;
- deterministic group order is preserved as `Opportunities` before `Risks`;
- each group may render at most three items;
- exact `Today's Top 3` candidate keys are excluded by contract;
- broader-related `Today's Top 3` conditions require independent supporting evidence;
- display states are locked as `Ready`, `Unavailable`, `Partial`, and `Error-safe`;
- error-safe text renders inline and non-modal;
- one-sentence `Why It Matters` text remains part of each prepared item;
- source authority and freshness labels remain explicit;
- status display remains plain-language and read-only;
- no fake opportunity, risk, market, attention, urgency, upside, or downside values are displayed.
