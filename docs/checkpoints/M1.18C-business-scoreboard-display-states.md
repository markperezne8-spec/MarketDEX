## Scope

M1.18C locks deterministic Business Scoreboard display-state behavior for the existing read-only Mission Control shell.

This checkpoint does not add runtime UI, real scoreboard values, formulas, financial providers, networking, polling, persistence, alerts, notifications, automation, commands, manual KPI inputs, or business mutation.

## Locked States

- `Ready` means prepared local evidence supports the read-only shell, group, or metric.
- `Unavailable` means required prepared evidence is missing.
- `Partial` means some prepared local evidence exists but the shell, group, or metric is incomplete.
- `Not applicable` means the selected period or evidence shape does not apply.
- `Error-safe` means assembly or injected evidence failed and the surface renders safe inline text.
