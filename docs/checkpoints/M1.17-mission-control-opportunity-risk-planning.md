# M1.17 Mission Control Opportunity + Risk Planning

**Status:** Planning checkpoint active  
**Canonical branch:** `main`  
**Source of truth:** GitHub repository `markperezne8-spec/MarketDEX`  
**Issue:** #509  
**Parent checkpoint:** `docs/checkpoints/M1.16E-capital-health-checkpoint-sync.md`

## Milestone intent

M1.17 defines the next safe Mission Control North Star milestone after the completed M1.16 `Capital Health` sequence: a contract-first path for a read-only `Opportunity + Risk` surface.

The future surface should eventually answer: `Where may the business gain or lose meaningful advantage if Mark waits?`

This planning checkpoint authorizes planning only. It does not implement UI, classify real opportunity or risk candidates, display fake values, poll market sources, send alerts, execute actions, mutate business data, or introduce live checks.

## Current gap from the North Star

M1.14 mapped `Opportunity + Risk` as a contract-first North Star region because no approved desktop opportunity/risk view-model exists yet.

The approved Opportunity + Risk direction exists in repository authority:

- Opportunity + Risk is concise situational awareness, not a second priority engine;
- it may display up to three eligible Opportunities and three eligible Risks;
- Today's Top 3 remains the sole force-ranked actionable attention surface;
- exact Today's Top 3 candidates are excluded;
- broader related conditions require independent supporting evidence;
- opportunities and risks must be explainable from identifiable evidence;
- Opportunity does not mean BUY, guaranteed upside, or probability of profit;
- a red presentation state alone does not create a Risk candidate.

## Safe planning principle

M1.17 treats Opportunity + Risk as an explanation contract, not as permission to invent market intelligence or alerts.

Future implementation may show real Opportunity + Risk items only after an immutable view-model or service boundary defines:

- the local-only evidence accepted by the surface;
- deterministic Opportunity and Risk item ordering;
- the exclusion rule for exact Today's Top 3 candidates;
- the evidence required before a broader related condition may remain visible;
- plain-language unavailable, partial, ready, and error-safe states;
- the required one-sentence Why It Matters explanation;
- direction or condition labels;
- future OPEN routing toward authoritative detail without turning Mission Control into an editor;
- how missing, stale, conflicting, or insufficient evidence renders honestly.

## Approved future evidence posture

M1.17 future slices may consume only prepared or injected local evidence. Missing evidence must produce `Unavailable` or `Partial` output rather than inferred opportunity, risk, urgency, or live status.

Candidate future evidence categories, once explicitly contracted:

- normalized Attention Candidate evidence already approved for application use;
- local inventory, listing readiness, order, exception, capital, grading, data trust, release readiness, seasonal window, or reconciliation evidence;
- prepared local market-observation snapshots already preserved in approved records;
- source authority, evidence confidence, observation date, and freshness labels;
- prepared OPEN routing targets to authoritative detail surfaces.

Excluded until separately approved:

- live market polling;
- marketplace or live-pricing integration;
- financial-provider integration;
- alerts or notifications;
- background workers or scheduled checks;
- Buy, Sell, grade, list, purchase, or ship commands;
- fake opportunity values, fake risk values, or fake attention evidence;
- probability of profit, guaranteed upside, or priority-score display.

## Planned deterministic state model

The future contract should preserve these states:

- `Ready` - sufficient prepared local evidence supports a read-only item or group;
- `Unavailable` - required evidence is missing;
- `Partial` - some local evidence exists, but the group or item cannot be treated as complete;
- `Error-safe` - injected evidence or assembly fails and the UI must render inline safe text.

State labels must be deterministic and plain-language. Error-safe rendering must remain inline and non-modal.

## Planned display contract

The future visible surface should remain compact and read-only.

Candidate groups:

- `Opportunities` - up to three conditions where waiting may reduce useful advantage;
- `Risks` - up to three conditions where delay may increase downside or reduce flexibility.

Candidate item fields:

- item kind: Opportunity or Risk;
- icon and concise label;
- one-sentence Why It Matters explanation;
- direction or condition indicator;
- evidence summary;
- source authority;
- freshness or as-of label when available;
- future OPEN routing target.

The display must not include action buttons, launch workflows, command execution, popups, alerts, notifications, automatic remediation, or direct mutation controls.

## Relationship to existing Mission Control surfaces

M1.17 should preserve existing accepted Mission Control order and behavior.

`Opportunity + Risk` should complement completed surfaces:

- `Today's Top 3` answers: `What deserves attention first today?`
- `Capital Health` answers: `What is the current capital condition of the business?`
- `Opportunity + Risk` answers: `Where may advantage or downside change if Mark waits?`

Future Opportunity + Risk values may inform future attention items only through a separately approved evidence contract.

## Suggested mergeable build sequence

### M1.17A - Opportunity + Risk view-model contract

Create a non-visual immutable view model for the `Opportunity + Risk` surface. It should accept prepared local evidence through injection only, preserve deterministic Opportunity/Risk ordering, exclude exact Today's Top 3 candidates by contract, and render missing evidence as `Unavailable` or `Partial`.

### M1.17B - First visible Opportunity + Risk shell

Place a compact read-only `Opportunity + Risk` surface in Mission Control using the M1.17A contract. Default missing evidence should render honest unavailable Opportunity and Risk groups. This visible build requires screenshot review.

### M1.17C - Deterministic display states

Lock `Ready`, `Unavailable`, `Partial`, and `Error-safe` visual rendering for Opportunity and Risk groups/items, including inline error-safe text and deterministic ordering.

### M1.17D - Visual and contract verification

Record screenshot evidence, placement verification, read-only behavior, injected-only behavior, deterministic ordering, exclusion from Today's Top 3, absence of action controls, and absence of popup, polling, networking, persistence, automation, alert, notification, provider, fake-value, Buy/Sell command, or business mutation paths.

### M1.17E - Checkpoint and documentation sync

Synchronize repository authority after accepted visual evidence and CI have passed.

Each build must remain independently reviewable and mergeable.
