# M1.19 planning - Mission Control Visual Intelligence

## Purpose

M1.19 defines the next guarded Mission Control North Star slice after the completed Business Scoreboard sequence.

Visual Intelligence is a read-only awareness region. It may explain prepared evidence, but it does not create evidence, calculate business outcomes, or initiate work.

## Authority

The approved Mission Control order is:

1. Today's Top 3
2. Capital Health
3. Opportunity + Risk
4. Business Scoreboard
5. Visual Intelligence

The existing M1.14 shell reserves this region below Business Scoreboard and above the later Inventory workspace content.

## Planned regions

When a separately approved implementation contract exists, Visual Intelligence may present these regions in stable order:

1. Performance charts
2. Inventory alerts
3. Attention heat map
4. Market attention trend

These names describe future contract areas. They do not authorize chart rendering, alert generation, ranking, trend calculation, or provider integration.

## Evidence boundary

- Evidence must be prepared and injected through an explicit local-only contract.
- Missing evidence renders honest Unavailable output.
- Incomplete evidence renders Partial output with the available evidence identified.
- Valid complete evidence may render Ready output.
- Provider, parser, formula, or adapter failures render Error-safe output without throwing through the visible boundary.
- No value, trend, alert, score, or chart point may be invented from absent evidence.
- The surface remains read-only and must not become an evidence authority.

## Deterministic behavior

The future contract must lock:

- region ordering;
- title and subtitle text;
- state labels and tone mapping;
- empty, missing, partial, and error-safe messages;
- evidence labels and source attribution;
- stable ordering within injected collections;
- explicit period or freshness labels when supplied by evidence.

The contract must reject invalid inputs deterministically and preserve immutable outputs after construction.

## Placement and visual gate

The existing Visual Intelligence shell remains after Business Scoreboard. M1.19 does not change runtime placement or styling.

Any future visible implementation must be a separate issue and draft PR. It requires:

- a screenshot at the accepted desktop layout;
- a responsive or resize check when layout behavior changes;
- confirmation that the region remains read-only;
- Mark visual acceptance before final merge.

## Explicit non-goals

M1.19 planning does not authorize:

- runtime UI changes;
- real charts or charting libraries;
- chart formulas, score formulas, heat-map formulas, or trend calculations;
- live marketplace, pricing, financial, or external providers;
- network access, polling, background workers, schedulers, or startup execution;
- persistence, migrations, caching, or database writes;
- alerts, notifications, automation, or task execution;
- action controls, commands, business mutation, or route changes;
- new dependencies, workspaces, registries, or parallel authorities;
- fake values or inferred business facts.

## Next build gate

The next implementation slice should be a non-visual immutable Visual Intelligence view-model contract. It must define the four regions, injected evidence shape, deterministic states, ordering, and error-safe behavior before any visible behavior is changed.

M1.19 planning changes documentation only. It requires no additional visual check.
