# Inventory Pricing Acceptance

Local acceptance after merge:

- Select an inventory asset with known quantity and total cost.
- Confirm Unit Cost.
- Choose marketplace and enter shipping/packaging.
- Change Target ROI from 20% to 40%; Recommended Price must rise.
- Click Use Recommended Price; Target Sale Price must equal Recommended Price.
- Confirm Sale Readiness and True Profit recalculate immediately.

This acceptance validates the operator decision workflow without recording a completed sale.
