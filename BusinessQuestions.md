## 🎯 Purpose

Business Questions define the problems MarketDEX exists to solve.

Every workspace, module, worksheet, report, chart, and future feature
must answer one or more meaningful business questions.

If a feature does not answer a meaningful business question, it should
be reconsidered.

The current active product scope is:

🃏 Pokémon TCG

🐉 Dragon Ball Card Game

🧸 Funko Pop

Questions should remain category-neutral whenever the business need is
shared.

Category-specific questions should exist only when a product type
genuinely requires them.
